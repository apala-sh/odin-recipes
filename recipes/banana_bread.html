Banana Bread

A type of bread prepared with mashed bananas, walnuts, maida/plain flour, and wheat mix. The texture of the bread is very similar to the eggless banana cake as it is moist, sweet and fluffy and is typically served for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 3 banana, ripped
  • ¾ cup sugar, or 1 cup depending on sweetness of banana
  • ½ cup oil or butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup maida / plain flour / all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup wheat flour / atta
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda / sodium bicarbonate
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon powder / dalchini powder
  • pinch of salt
  • ½ cup walnut / akharot

Instructions

  1. firstly, take ripped bananas and cut them into pieces.
  2. also add sugar and mash the banana. furthermore, add oil and vanilla extract.
  3. also sieve maida, wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder and salt.
  4. blend and combine all the dry ingredients with the banana puree.
  5. furthermore, add walnut and fold gently. transfer the cake batter into the cake mould or bread mould.
  6. pat the tray twice to remove the air incorporated into the batter.
  7. place the tray into preheated oven. bake the cake at 180 degree celsius or 356 degree fahrenheit for 40 minutes.
  8. further, allow the cake to cool completely. and later cut to slices and serve.
  9. finally, serve the eggless & vegan banana bread or store in airtight container.
